04 / About

Background

I'm a Mechatronics Engineer who spent 12 years at the heart of automotive electronics manufacturing — designing, building, and owning the production systems that put vehicles on the road. My core work was a vehicle commissioning platform spanning multiple clients, servers, shopfloor devices, and inter-system integrations across facilities in Mexico, Germany, and China.

That work made me fluent in the full stack of industrial technology: from embedded systems and automotive protocols (CAN, DoIP) up through networks, RFID, IoT, and into the software and data layer. I've led international technical teams, managed supplier relationships, and delivered projects under OEM-level scrutiny.

Now I'm building at the intersection of manufacturing expertise and AI — applying generative AI and intelligent automation to the exact kinds of production problems I've lived for a decade. I'm based in Barcelona and open to senior remote roles or local opportunities with international scope.
